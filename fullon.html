Nauči se avtomacijo na pravi način
Nazaj domov

1. Splošna pomoč & Get-WinEvent

Get-Help
Get-Help Get-WinEvent

2. Skrite datoteke na namizju

Get-ChildItem $env:USERPROFILE\Desktop -Hidden

3. Izpis vseh ukazov

Get-Command

4. Storitve z nizom "time"

Get-Service -Name *time*

5. Samo delujoče storitve

Get-Service | Where-Object Status -eq "Running"

6. Status & startup w32time

Get-Service -Name W32time | Select-Object Status, StartType

7. Testni zagon W32time (WhatIf)

Start-Service -Name W32time -WhatIf

8. Zagon, izpis in ustavitev W32time

Start-Service -Name W32time -Confirm
Get-Service -Name W32time | Select-Object Name, Status, StartType
Stop-Service -Name W32time -Confirm

9. Ponovni zagon W32time

Restart-Service -Name W32time -Confirm

10. Startup Disabled za w32time

Set-Service -Name W32time -StartupType Disabled -Confirm

11. Ustavi, spremeni na Manual & izpiši

Stop-Service -Name W32time
Set-Service -Name W32time -StartupType Manual
Get-Service -Name W32time | Select-Object Name, Status, StartType

12. Ime procesa Notepad++

Get-Process -Name notepad++ | Select-Object ProcessName

13. Podatki o procesu Notepad++

Get-Process -Name notepad++ | Select-Object Id, WS, StartTime

14. Procesi z manj kot 500 MB RAM

Get-Process | Where-Object { $_.WS -lt 500MB }

15. Zapri Notepad++ po ID

Stop-Process -Id 12345 -Confirm

16. Zagon Notepad++ z izbiro uporabnika

Start-Process notepad++ -Credential (Get-Credential)

17. Podatki o fizičnih mrežnih karticah

Get-NetAdapter -Physical | Select-Object Name, Description, InterfaceIndex

18. IPv4 naslov mrežne kartice

Get-NetAdapter -Physical |
Get-NetIPAddress -AddressFamily IPv4 |
Select-Object IPAddress

19. MAC & hitrost mrežne kartice

Get-NetAdapter -Physical | Select-Object Name, MacAddress, LinkSpeed

20. Ponovni zagon mrežne kartice

Restart-NetAdapter -Name "Ethernet" -Confirm -PassThru

21. Vrste log datotek

Get-WinEvent -ListLog *

22. Vnosi loga Security (paging)

Get-EventLog -LogName Security | Out-Host -Paging

23. Zadnjih 10 vnosov Security

Get-EventLog -LogName Security -Newest 10

24. Zadnjih 5 vnosov Security (tabela)

Get-EventLog -LogName Security -Newest 5 |
Select-Object Index, TimeWritten, EntryType, InstanceId, Message |
Format-Table -AutoSize

25. FailureAudit vnosi

Get-EventLog -LogName Security -EntryType FailureAudit

26. Branje vegova.log

Get-Content "$env:USERPROFILE\Desktop\vegova.log"
Get-Content "$env:USERPROFILE\Desktop\vegova.log" -TotalCount 2
Get-Content "$env:USERPROFILE\Desktop\vegova.log" -Tail 2
